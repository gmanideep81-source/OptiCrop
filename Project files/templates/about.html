About the project

Data-driven decisions for every plot of land.

OptiCrop is a machine learning-based agricultural recommendation system built to help farmers, researchers, and agricultural policymakers make smarter, evidence-based decisions about what to plant and where.

By analyzing Nitrogen, Phosphorous, and Potassium levels alongside temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall, the system identifies patterns between environmental conditions and crop success — patterns that are often too complex or subtle for manual analysis alone.

The result is a tool that supports three groups: farmers looking for the best crop to plant right now, researchers exploring crop-environment relationships, and policymakers shaping sustainable agricultural strategy.
